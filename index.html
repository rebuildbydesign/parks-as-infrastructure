Parks as Essential Climate Infrastructure

Parks can serve multiple purposes—flood protection, community recreation, improving air quality—maximizing their value as multi-benefit investments. This map highlights parks in New York City as essential climate infrastructure.

Methodology

Rebuild analyzed 2,385 City and State-Owned Parks from TPL’s database to identify parks on or near current and future stormwater and storm surge flood zones. A 100-foot buffer was applied to show how flooding in or near parks can impact nearby neighborhoods and infrastructure. This study highlights an opportunity to secure funding for flood mitigation that reduces risks while improving parks for recreation and resilience.
